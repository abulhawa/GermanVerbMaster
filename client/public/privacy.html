Overview

GermanVerbMaster ("the App") is a German language learning application for Android, developed by Ali Abulhawa (qortxai.com). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Summary: GermanVerbMaster does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All progress and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data (coarse or precise)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Financial or payment information

Local Data Storage

Any data generated through your use of the App — such as quiz scores, practice history, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data in your Android device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

Internet Access

If the App requests internet access, it is used solely for the following optional purposes:

  • Loading verb or grammar content from our servers
  • Checking for application updates (where applicable)

No personal data is transmitted during any of these operations. Requests are made anonymously and do not include any device or user identifiers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or user tracking libraries (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or similar services).

If you downloaded the App from the Google Play Store, Google's own Privacy Policy governs any data Google collects as part of their platform. The developer has no access to any data Google may collect independently.

Children's Privacy

GermanVerbMaster is a general-purpose language learning tool suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data from any users, the App is safe for children to use. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
